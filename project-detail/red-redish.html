Red Redish

Project Type

Restaurant

Location

San Francisco, CA

Completed

2022

Rad Radish draws inspiration from Northern California’s rich design and craft culture, from Sea Ranch to the 1960s–70s local craft movement. The interior highlights locally made tiles, handwoven elements, and murals by a Bay Area artist reflecting California landscapes. Bathrooms nod to Sea Ranch design principles, while natural materials, artisanal details, and a thoughtful composition of textures create a contemporary yet distinctly Californian aesthetic.
