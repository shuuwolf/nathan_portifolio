Roof 106

Project Type

Restaurant

Location

Healdsburg, CA

Completed

2021

Roof 106 crowns The Matheson with a rooftop bar and lounge infused with classic Northern California bohemian style. Natural materials, handcrafted furnishings, and relaxed seating create an earthy, eclectic atmosphere. Lush greenery, warm wood tones, and artisanal details celebrate the local landscape and makers. Designed for both intimate gatherings and lively evenings, the space offers sweeping views of Healdsburg Plaza while embodying the relaxed sophistication of California wine country.
