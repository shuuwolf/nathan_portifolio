Vrai

Project Type

Retail

Location

Los Angeles, CA

Completed

2021

VRAI Los Angeles showcases a commitment to sustainable design through thoughtfully sourced materials. Neolith natural stone flooring, clay plaster walls, and low-VOC paints improve air quality, while allergen-friendly carpets and locally made furniture reduce environmental impact. Custom Eames chairs, solar-powered benches, and LED lighting emphasize longevity and eco-conscious craftsmanship. The design balances modern sophistication with green principles, creating a luxurious yet environmentally responsible nightclub experience.
