About Nathan

Nathan is an interior designer with over a decade of experience creating distinctive restaurants and residences in San Francisco, New York, and beyond. Drawing from a background in architecture and a deep appreciation for culture, he designs spaces that are both functional and emotionally resonant. His work blends global inspiration with a grounded understanding of how people live, gather, and experience interiors.

Originally from Southern California, Nathan earned his Bachelor of Architecture with a minor in Sustainable Environments from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo, and later a Master’s in Interior Architecture from the University of Oregon. A formative year studying in Copenhagen immersed him in European design and travel, shaping his refined yet adventurous aesthetic. His career has spanned theater and film set design, freelance interiors, and extensive travel throughout the U.S. and abroad—experiences that inform his layered, culturally aware approach to every project.
