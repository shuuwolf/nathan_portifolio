About Nathan

Nathan is an interior designer with over a decade of experience shaping distinctive spaces across the restaurant, hospitality, and residential sectors. His work spans both coasts and beyond, blending architectural precision with an appreciation for authentic culture. He designs interiors that are functional yet emotionally resonant, guided by a thoughtful understanding of how people live, gather, and connect.

Originally from Southern California, Nathan holds a Bachelor of Architecture with a minor in Sustainable Environments from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo, and a Master’s in Interior Architecture from the University of Oregon. A formative year studying in Copenhagen immersed him in European design and travel, shaping an aesthetic that is refined, layered, adventurous, and culturally aware.
